Junior Front End Developer

Work Schedule

The basic work schedule of a junior front end developer is:

Write supporting code, design, build, and maintain websites , backup files from web sites to local directories for recovery in case of problemes, select programming languages, design tools or applications, evaluate code to make sure it is properly structured, meets industry standards and is compatible with browsers, devices, or operating systems, attend daily meets that help make sure tasks are completed on schedule, communicating with other developers/leader and cross communication with managers and designers

Flexibility

Being a front end developer is like most 9 to 5 jobs when it comes to flexibility. But, because most of what you do is on a computer, many jobs allow you to work at home, which can add more flexibility.

Advancing

After about five years of experience in a front end development department a junior front end developer can advance in their career by becoming a senior front end developer.

Money

With a junior front end developer salary can cover living expenses for a single person and comfortably support a family especially if there is another source of income. You would also have spending money which can be used for enjoyment like eating out, entertainment, vacations, etc.

Senior UX/UI Developer

Work Schedule

Overall Senior UX/UX Developer's role is to create user-friendly interfaces that enable users to understand how to use complex technical products.

The normal work schedule of a senior UX / UI is:
The normal work schedule of a senior UX/UI developer is: Design websites or web applications, develop specifications or procedures for website development or maintenance, update website content, collaborate with others to resolve information technology issues, conduct research to gain information about products or processes, communicate with network personnel or Web site hosting agencies to address hardware or software issues affecting Web sites.

Flexibility

Just like a front end developer, most of what you do is on a computer so many jobs allow you to work at home, which can add more flexibility.

Advancing

At least 10+ years of working experience as a UX/UI developer you could advance to a lead UX/UI position.

Money

The salary median for a four-person household in the US is $68,400 per year so with a salary range of 100,000 - 135,000 per year you could not only comfortably live and support a family, you could purchase many things you want. For example, entertainment, cars, vacations, etc.

Project Manager Specialists

Work Schedule

The normal work schedule of a Project Manager is:

Assign duties or responsibilities to project personnel, communicate with key stakeholders to determine project requirements and objectives, confer with project personnel to identify and resolve problems, create project status presentations for delivery to customers or project personnel, develop or update project plans including information such as objectives, technologies, schedules, funding, and staffing.

Flexibility

Many project manager positions offer unlimited paid time off. Because it is a project manager's job to create and update a project plan you can evenly spread out projects and stagger the start dates of projects if needed. This makes your schedule more consistent and life less stressful. On the other hand as a lead role you are in charge of a team of people which can be very demanding.

At least 5-10+ years of working experience as a Project Manager you could advance to a Senior Project Manager position.
